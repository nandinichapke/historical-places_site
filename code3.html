3 AJANTA CAVES State: Maharashtra, India. District: Aurangabad. Nearby Town: Ajanta village (about 12 km away). Indian Citizens: ₹50. SAARC/BIMSTEC Visitors: ₹540. Foreigners/NRIs: ₹1100. Mausoleum Access (Optional): Extra ₹200 MTDC Foothill Restaurant: The main option at the entrance, offering Indian food, Thalis, and juices, with separate seating for veg/non-veg during lunch; good for convenience. Hotel Ajanta Green: A clean spot about 3 km away, good for a quick meal or stay. Agrawals Restaurant & Lodging: Located in Fardapur (nearby village) for basic Indian food. Organized Tours: Many companies (like Trikon, Mumbai Pune Adventures) offer daily/weekend packages from Pune/Mumbai including transport, guides, and sometimes Ellora Caves & Grishneshwar Temple.
